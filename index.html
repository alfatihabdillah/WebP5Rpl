PT GarudaFood History

Founded in 1990, PT GarudaFood has grown into a leading company in Indonesia. Committed to quality and innovation, we continue to introduce products that are loved by consumers.

1990 - Foundation

PT GarudaFood was established with a vision to provide quality food products to Indonesian consumers.

1995 - Product Expansion

Expanded product line to include a variety of snacks and beverages, becoming a household name in Indonesia.

2005 - International Expansion

Began exporting products to neighboring countries in Southeast Asia, marking the beginning of international presence.

2020 - Modern Innovation

Implemented advanced manufacturing technologies and sustainable practices, reinforcing commitment to quality and environmental responsibility.
